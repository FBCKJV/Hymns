Tips & Help
Get the most out of FBC Hymns & Spiritual Songs
Listening
♫Tap the mini player at the bottom to expand it into a full view with large artwork, lyrics, and easy-to-tap controls.
↻Tap the shuffle icon to play songs in random order within the current library tab.
ATurn on AUTO to automatically continue to the next hymn when one finishes — great for hands-free listening during devotions.
Lyrics & Search
♪Tap the lyrics icon in the player to follow along verse by verse while a hymn plays. Not every recording has lyrics yet — more are added regularly.
🔍Use the search bar to find hymns by title or theme — try searching "grace," "comfort," or "heaven."
Favorites & Downloads
♡Tap the heart on any hymn to save it to Favorites for quick access — this does not save it for offline use.
↓Tap the download icon to save a hymn’s audio to your device so it plays without an internet connection. A teal checkmark means it’s downloaded.
⚠Downloaded songs use storage space on your device. Only download the hymns you know you’ll want offline — everything else streams normally and uses no storage.

Sharing
🔗Tap Share on any hymn to send it to a friend or family member’s phone in one tap.
Install as an App
iPhone & iPad (Safari)
1. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow)
2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
3. Tap Add — the app icon appears on your home screen
Android (Chrome)
1. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top right
2. Tap Add to Home screen or Install app

About the Archives
All recordings are public domain or freely licensed for ministry use

🎼 Olmstead Baptist Church Hymnal

Live congregational singing recordings in the public domain. All 150+ hymns freely reproducible. Hosted at baptistarchive.com and olmsteadbaptistchurch.org.

🎹 Small Church Music

Founded in 2006 by Rev. Clyde McLennan (1941–2022), an ordained Baptist pastor from Australia. Nearly 15,000 public-domain MP3s — pipe organ, piano, small band — recorded as a free gift to congregations worldwide. See smallchurchmusic.com.

🎵 Bible Baptist Church DeLand — Piano Project

Live piano recordings from congregational singing at THE BIBLE Baptist Church of DeLand, FL (Pastor James W. Knox). Great Hymns of the Faith, IFB. See jameswknox.org.

📖 Sung Psalms — Scottish Metrical Psalter (1650)

Acappella psalm singing from Reformed and Free Church congregations. Over 700 recordings at ThePsalmsSung.org. Recordings used with permission of the Free Church of Scotland.

🎶 Genevan Psalter

The historic 1562 Genevan tunes (Bourgeois/Marot/Beza) sung by Reformed congregations. These tunes have accompanied metrical Psalm singing since the Reformation.

📻 Internet Archive — Baptist Music

Public domain Baptist and gospel hymn recordings from the Internet Archive's open collections at archive.org.

ℹ️ About This App

FBC Hymns & Songs — companion to the FBC KJV Bible App (fbckjv.app). Favorites and listening history are stored locally on your device. Audio streams directly from the source archives.
