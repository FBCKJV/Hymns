🔊 Audio Bible — Two Ways to Listen
📖Book Play — From any book's chapter list, tap the 🔊 Listen button below the chapters to stream the entire book. A floating player bar stays with you across all screens — read along, search, or navigate while the audio continues. Your position in every book is saved automatically.
🔊Chapter Play — While reading, tap the 🔊 button next to the chapter title to hear just that chapter. Audio plays from the chapter start to the end of the last verse, then stops automatically. Navigate to another chapter and tap 🔊 again to continue reading aloud.
▶Both modes share the same floating player bar with play/pause, ◀30 / 30▶ skip, speed control (0.5×–2.0×), and a progress bar. Chapter Play shows chapter progress; Book Play shows whole-book progress.
💾Book Play positions are saved to the cloud with your restore code — switch devices and pick up exactly where you left off in every book. Chapter Play does not save position since it always starts at the chapter beginning.
☁️ Backup & Restore Your Data
🔑Your data is automatically backed up to the cloud. Tap My Restore Code to see your unique 6-character code — write it down or screenshot it. On a new device or browser, tap Enter Code and your streaks, badges, bookmarks and progress are fully restored.
